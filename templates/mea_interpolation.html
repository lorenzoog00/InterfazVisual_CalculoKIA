MEA-CO2 Equilibrium Curve

The equilibrium curve represents the relationship between CO2 concentration in the liquid phase (MEA) and the partial pressure of CO2 in the gas phase at specific temperature and MEA concentration.

This curve is crucial for understanding MEA's absorption capacity and designing efficient CO2 capture processes.
